The Best Movies According to Dylan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Donnie Darko

Great Sci-Fi movie with an addictive story. I purposely avoid watching this movie so it never gets old.

Avengers: Endgame

The greatest superhero movie of all time, tying all previous Marvel Movies together.

Hereditary

Arguably the most artistic and genuinely scary horror movies of our current time.
